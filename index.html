harmonizing adventure and serenity into a single destination.

sumbawa known as

Tana Samawa, is very rich in crops that are processed by the people and served in a variety of unique and authentic food and beverages. Sumbawa Island is an island located in the province of West Nusa Tenggara, Indonesia with its largest city being Bima. The island has its highest point at Mount Tambora which is an active volcano. Sumbawa has a larger area than Bali and Lombok combined. On this island, many activities can be done such as surfing, exploring the jungle, snorkeling off the coast, and seeing ancient stone sarcophagi. Travelers also stay at the best inns in Sumbawa, although accommodation is fairly standard unless staying on Moyo Island, which is mostly used by the wealthy due to its luxurious and remote location.

Tambora Mountain

4,5/5

Mount Tambora, volcanic mountain on the northern coast of Sumbawa island, Indonesia. It is 2,851 metres (9,354 feet) high.

Kenawa Island

4,7/5

Kenawa Island is a small uninhabited island in the Gili Balu' group, which is included in West Sumbawa Regency, West Nusa Tenggara (NTB) Province.

Lawar Beach

4,6/5

Lawar is a wild beach in the southwest of Sumbawa island, Indonesia. The beach is located inside a protected bay featuring scenic hills around.

Satonda Island

4,6/5

Satonda is an island in West Nusa Tenggara province of Indonesia. It is off the north coast of Sumbawa island. The Island is located in Dompu Regency, 3 km from Sanggar Strait in the Flores Sea.
